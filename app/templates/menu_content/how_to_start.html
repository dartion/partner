How to use the website?




1.     Register by signing up here with your email ID and phone number.
2.     Administrator will approve your account
3.     Login with your registered username and password. Please click on Forgot Password link if you don't remember your password
4.    
Once you're logged in, you can search for profiles under "Search Profiles" tab or you can create profile(s) and click on "Submit".
There will be account details displayed under the "Profile Status" tab once you have submitted the profile.
5.    
Administrator will then approve the profile after the fee has been received.
6.     To deactivate/delete your account/profile, please contact administrator.
